---
title: Map
description: A navigable atlas of your Zotero library.
---

The **Map** turns your indexed Zotero papers into a navigable graph. Papers are placed by
similarity and connected by shared citations, so you can see the structure of a literature at
a glance - clusters, bridges, and outliers.

It runs entirely on your machine and needs **no model**: placement, clustering, and labels are
computed deterministically from the papers' content and reference links.

## Using it

- **Scope it** by selecting papers (or whole collections) in the sidebar - the map redraws to
  just that set.
- **Open a paper** from the map to read it, with citations that point to the exact source page.

The more of your library you index, the richer the map. Indexing is additive, so you can grow
it over time.
